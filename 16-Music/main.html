Music Visualized

Our auditory experience is so distinct from our visual experience as it can never be grasped as a whole. While one can quickly get a sense of the rough compositional proportion and geometric relationship by glancing at Mondrian's paintings, the contrapuntal complexity and rhythmic correlation in Bach's 'The Art of Fugue' only unfold with time. This music visualization project tries to build an integrated auditory and visual experience by offering a new way to appreciate musical masterpieces.
